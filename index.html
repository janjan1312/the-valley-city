About The Valley City

Located in the vibrant community of Quezon City, Metro Manila, The Valley City is a premier destination built by pickleball enthusiasts, for pickleball enthusiasts. Whether you are stepping onto the court for the very first time or you are a seasoned pro preparing for a tournament, our state-of-the-art facility is designed to elevate your game. We believe in the power of community, staying active, and the sheer joy of a perfectly executed dink. Join us for open play, rent a private court, or participate in one of our upcoming local leagues!
